Cookies and Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us recognize you, remember your preferences, and understand how you use our site. Cookies are essential for providing a personalized and efficient browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • •
    Essential Cookies:

    Required for basic website functionality, security, and user authentication.

  • •
    Performance Cookies:

    Help us understand how visitors use our website and identify areas for improvement.

  • •
    Functional Cookies:

    Remember your preferences and personalize your experience on our website.

  • •
    Marketing Cookies:

    Track your activity to display targeted advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness.

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • • Delete existing cookies from your device
  • • Block cookies from being stored on your device
  • • Receive notifications when cookies are being set

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website and your ability to access certain features.

We use third-party analytics services to track and analyze website usage. These services may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing activities. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services to understand their data practices.

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our security practices include:
